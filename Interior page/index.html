Product Care

- Handle your vape device with care to avoid damage to delicate internal components from drops or falls, which can affect functionality and susceptibility to dust and moisture.

- Immediately wipe up e-liquid spills, splashes, or drips to prevent buildup and maintain the appearance and cleanliness of your vape.

- Keep replacement parts like coils on hand to ensure uninterrupted vaping experiences, and consider setting up auto-ship subscriptions for convenience.

- Regularly clean your vape tank, ideally every two weeks or when switching e-juice flavors, using warm water, a few drops of dish soap, and a clean towel.

- Replace coils as needed based on factors like burnt taste, reduced vapor production, and flavor quality.
